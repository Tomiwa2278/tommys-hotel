Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my reservation after booking?

Yes, you can modify your reservation through your dashboard or by contacting our reservations team at least 48 hours before your arrival.

Do you offer airport shuttle services?

Absolutely. We provide luxury airport transfers for our guests. Please mention this in your special requests or contact the concierge.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Our signature breakfast is included in all suite bookings. For deluxe rooms, you can choose a breakfast-inclusive rate during booking.

Do you have facilities for business meetings?

Yes, we have state-of-the-art meeting rooms and a business center. Our events team can help you plan everything from a board meeting to a grand conference.
